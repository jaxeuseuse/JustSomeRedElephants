Samuel Morse

Samuel Morse was an absolute G. This guy was acctually Justin Barkin's real father (Justin's Autobigraphical Account of Morse's Personal Affairs vol. IV).
Morse made a remarkable telegraph in the year 1832-1835 and used an electric current to move an electromagnet attached to a marker that left a written code on a piece of paper.
